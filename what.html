What are Portal Guns?

Portal Guns are a fictional item from popular games Portal and Portal2. Portal guns, as their names suggest, allow the player to create small portals to complete various tasks. By changing the gun settings to the orange portal, the player is able to shoot said portal to complete the set and allow the player to use the portals. In game, the player uses the portals to complete several tests that include:

  • moving the player to a point otherwise impossible to go to
  • transporting objects, like cubes, to the player or elsewhere
  • transporting liquids that allow items and the player to
    • bounce
    • speed up
  • the careful placement of a bridge made of light
  • redirecting lazerbeams
